About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania, pursuing a Doctoral degree in Computer and Information Science under the supervision of Prof. Insup Lee. I am interested in pursuing research in areas of Distributed Systems, Security and Privacy. Currently, I am working with Prof. Insup Lee in designing and developing a Secure Logger for Medical Devices. I am also involved in other projects involving the Data Distribution Service (DDS).
